TracetoForge: Turn Any Tool Photo Into a Custom-Fit Insert

The fastest way to create custom tool storage. Snap a photo of any tool, auto-trace the outline, and export STL, 3MF, SVG, or DXF files for 3D printing, laser cutting, or CNC machining. Works with Milwaukee Packout, Gridfinity, DeWalt ToughSystem, Ridgid Pro Gear, and any toolbox. No CAD skills needed.

How It Works

1. Snap a photo of your tool on paper. 2. Auto-trace finds the outline. 3. Preview in 3D. 4. Export STL, 3MF, SVG, or DXF.

Insert Modes

Custom Tray, Gridfinity Bin (42mm grid), and 3D Object export. All modes support STL, 3MF, SVG, and DXF.

Compatible Systems

Milwaukee Packout, DeWalt ToughSystem, DeWalt TSTAK, Ridgid Pro Gear, Makita MakTrak, Flex Stack Pack, Gridfinity, Klein ModBox, Bosch L-Boxx, Festool Systainer, Kobalt, Husky, Craftsman, Stanley FatMax, Harbor Freight, Snap-on.
